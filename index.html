About Me

I am a mechanical engineering student at The Cooper Union specializing in mechanical design, autonomous systems, and controls. My project experience, which includes developing drone swarm control technology and competing with autonomous vehicles, has sharpened my skills in CAD design, programming, and collaborative problem-solving. My international background and multilingual abilities provide me with a unique perspective on teamwork and innovation. My goal is to apply this blend of technical skill and global insight to engineer creative solutions for real-world challenges.
